The Emperor Penguin

They are the biggest penguin of the penguin species.

Here are some facts about emperor penguins: Habitat: Ice shelves, fast ice platforms, sea for feeding Location: Antarctica Lifespan: 15 to 20 years Size: 45 inches tall Weight: 55 to 100 lb Color: Chicks are black and grey and covered in down. Adult penguins have black, white, and orange feathers Diet: Krill, fish, squid Predators: Killer whales, leopard seals, giant fulmars
