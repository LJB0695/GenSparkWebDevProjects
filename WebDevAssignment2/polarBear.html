The Polar Bear

They are the world's largest land predators.

Here are some facts about polar bears: Weight: Males weigh up to 1,700 pounds and females weigh up to 850 pounds Length/Height: When males stand on their back legs, they can reach heights of 10 feet Life span: 15-18 years in the wild, 30 years in human care Diet: Carnivore Range: U.S. (Alaska) Canada, Russia, Greenland, and Norway
